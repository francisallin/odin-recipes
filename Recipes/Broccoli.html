Broccoli

Desctiption

This is a simple and healthy dish that everyone can make.

Ingredients

  • Broccoli
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in a pot.
  2. Add garic into pot and boil for 5 minutes. Dice the garlic afterwards.
  3. Add broccoli into pot and boil for 5 minutes.
  4. Put boiled broccoli on dish and mix well with diced garlic. Season with salt if necessary.
