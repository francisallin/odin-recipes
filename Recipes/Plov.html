Plov

Desctiption

This is a common dish in Central Asian families, made of beef or lamb.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Beef
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Spice

Steps

  1. Pan fry the diced onion and carrots with spice.
  2. Add beef to the pan, cook for 5 minutes.
  3. Add water and rice, turn to low heat and slow cook for 20 minutes.
