Steamed Fish

Desctiption

This is a common dish in southern China families.

Ingredients

  • Fresh Fish
  • Ginger
  • Spring onions
  • Oil
  • Soy sauce

Steps

  1. Bring water to boil in wok and place a steaming rack above the water.
  2. Place fish and ginger on dish and put the dish on the rack. Cover the wok and steam for 10 minutes.
  3. Put spring onion on the fish and pour heated soy sauce and oil on the top.
